You need to avoid things that gobble up your time and make you inefficient. Some common time wasters include:
Telephone calls
Socializing
Poor communication
Indecision or procrastination
No co-ordination or teamwork

Tips for avoiding time wasters:

  • Don't be a slave to your telephone.
  • Shorten the amount of time taken up by unannounced visitors.
  • Know when to ask for help.
