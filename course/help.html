Click the Menu button to view a listing of every screen in this course. You can then access the screens in any order.

Use the Audio On/Off button to turn the course audio on and off.

Click the Transcript button to view the audio content being narrated for a particular screen.

Click the Help button to open the Help Screen.

Click the Replay button to revisit the current screen.

Use this toggle button to Play and Pause the audio or animation on a screen.

Click the Back button whenever you want to go back to the previous screen.

The Page Counter progress indicator shows your progress through the course.

Click the Next button when you have completed viewing a screen and are ready to move on to the next screen.
